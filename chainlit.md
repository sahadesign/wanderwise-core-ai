# 🌍 WanderWise AI
### Your Agentic Travel Companion

WanderWise is an autonomous travel agent designed to help you discover hidden gems tailored to your specific "vibe." Powered by **Multi-Agent Orchestration**, we cross-reference weather, geography, and local sentiment in real-time to build your perfect journey.

---

### ✨ Features
* **Vibe-Driven**: Choose between Nature, Spiritual, Historical, or Adventure.
* **Agentic Logic**: If a match isn't found, the agent autonomously expands its search radius.
* **Real-time Data**: Integrated with Google, Geoapify, and OpenWeather APIs.
